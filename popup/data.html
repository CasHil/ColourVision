ColourVision

By Casper Hildebrand

ColourVision's most important value is transparency. Therefore, we'd like to share exactly what information the extension collects, and why.

What? What data? Why?
Crash information Your Google Chrome version and the errors that the extension gives you. To understand why the extension is not working on your device and to be able to fix it.
Selected filter The type of colourblindness that you have selected. When you launch Chrome again, the extension will remember which one you last used.
